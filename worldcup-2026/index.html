What the numbers mean
Avg Rank
Average FIFA world ranking of this player's teams that are still in (lower = stronger). After the tournament it reverts to the average of all their drawn teams.
AGS — Advancing from Group Stage
How many of their teams are currently in a qualifying spot (top 2 of a group, or one of the 8 best third-placed teams), as it stands.
GSMR — Group Stage Matches Remaining
Group games their teams still have to play, out of 27 (9 teams × 3 games each).
R32 / R16 / QF / SF / Final
After the group stage, the matches their teams have left to play in the current knockout round.
Teams left
How many of their teams are still in the tournament. In the knockouts it turns green if you hold more than your even share of the teams still in (total remaining ÷ players) and red if fewer.
